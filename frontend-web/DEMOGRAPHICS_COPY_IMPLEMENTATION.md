## üéì Thesis Defense Talking Points

If questioned about language choices:

1. **"Tell Us a Bit About You"**
   - Reduces participant anxiety compared to clinical language
   - Follows best practices in UX writing for health tech

2. **"Health Context" not "Medical Information"**
   - Positions data collection as supportive, not diagnostic
   - Ethically appropriate for non-clinical research

3. **Emoji Icons**
   - Improves scannability and engagement (proven in UX studies)
   - Makes form feel less intimidating for Gen Z/Millennial users

4. **Helper Text on Every Field**
   - Transparency = ethical data collection
   - Informed consent requires clear purpose explanation

5. **"Screening" not "Diagnosis"**
   - Legally and ethically necessary distinction
   - Protects both researcher and participant

6. **Optional Blood Donation Section**
   - Shows respect for participant autonomy
   - Demonstrates ethical research design

7. **Multiple Privacy Reassurances**
   - Builds trust = higher completion rates
   - Follows GDPR/privacy best practices
